About

Instructional design meets real-world complexity.

I'm a training and instructional design specialist with a background that spans automotive dealership operations, healthcare, real estate, and customer service — bringing a rare ability to translate complex, high-stakes workflows into learning experiences that actually move people forward.

Currently serving as Software Training Specialist at Atlantic Coast Automotive Group, where I own DMS (Saturn) curriculum development from discovery through deployment. I design for how adults actually learn: context-first, outcome-driven, visually clear.

I bring AI tools into every stage of the design process — from rapid content prototyping with Claude and NotebookLM, to building synthetic practice environments with Synthesia — without losing the human judgment that makes training land.
